## 📖 About
ArchiWIZARD 2026 is a specialized Windows/MacOS desktop application designed for architects, engineers, and 3D designers who need rapid parametric modeling and automated drafting tools. It accelerates repetitive tasks in architectural design, such as generating floor plans, structural grids, and section cuts, without replacing professional CAD software.

## ⚙️ Requirements
- **Operating System**: Windows 10/11 (64-bit) or macOS 11 Big Sur+
- **Processor**: Intel Core i5-8400 or Apple M1 (or newer)
- **Memory**: 8 GB RAM minimum (16 GB recommended)
- **Disk Space**: 500 MB  space
- **Internet**: Required for initial activation and updates
- **Dependencies**: .NET Framework 4.8 (Windows) or Rosetta 2 (Apple Silicon Mac)

## ✨ Features
**Parametric Floor Plans** 🏗️ — Generate multi-level floor plans from simple boundary inputs with automatic room labeling and dimensioning.

**Structural Grid Designer** 📐 — Create and edit column grids, beam layouts, and foundation patterns with snap-to-rules constraints.

**Section & Elevation Auto-Cut** ✂️ — Extract sections and elevations from 3D models in seconds, with customizable cut lines and annotation styles.

**Material Takeoff Estimator** 📊 — Automatically calculate quantities of concrete, steel, glass, and finishes from your design model.

**IFC Import/Export** 🔄 — Seamlessly exchange BIM data with industry-standard IFC files, supporting IFC2x3 and IFC4.

**One-Click PDF Reports** 📄 — Generate professional design documentation with title blocks, revision clouds, and drawing lists.

**Custom  Engine** 🧩 — Automate sequences using Lua-based macros; no compilation required.

**DXF/DWG Compatibility** 🖋️ — Open and save drawings in AutoCAD formats up to 2026, preserving layer and entity data.

## ❓ FAQ
**Q: Is ArchiWIZARD 2026 safe to use in a production environment?**  
A: Yes, when used for its intended purpose of accelerating parametric design. It does not modify any existing software. Always maintain backups of your work.

**Q: I receive a "Library not found" error on macOS. How do I fix it?**  
A: Ensure Rosetta 2 is installed (`softwareupdate --install-rosetta`). Also verify that your Mac meets the minimum OS requirement (11 Big Sur or later).

**Q: Can I use ArchiWIZARD on a network drive or cloud folder?**  
A: Yes, but for best performance we recommend installing locally on an SSD. Cloud sync may cause slight delays when loading large IFC files.
